About Me

I am currently a PhD student at BBNC Lab of Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Xiangyang Ji. I got my BE degree from Department of Automation), School of Information Science and Technology, Tsinghua University) in 2016. My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Currently, I focus on 6D object pose estimation with RGB based methods. I am also interested in other vision based perception and localization problems in both 2D and 3D world.
